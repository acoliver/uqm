## Purpose

This is a narrow feasibility spike that converts hidden prerequisites into explicit deliverables before orbit/lifecycle implementation hardens around them. It does not complete the final FFI bridge; it establishes the exact dispatch-wrapper, navigation-global accessor, and persistence-window assumptions that P10/P11/P12 must share.

## Requirements Implemented (Expanded)

### REQ-PSS-FFI-004: Early dispatch-wrapper feasibility
**Requirement text**: The plan shall establish the concrete wrapper strategy for override/fallback, data-provider, and side-effect generation handlers before orbit and lifecycle phases depend on it.

### REQ-PSS-PERSIST-010: Persistence-window boundary feasibility
**Requirement text**: The legal get/put call sites and their host-owned lifecycle boundaries shall be validated as explicit implementation inputs before orbit/lifecycle coding hardens.

### REQ-PSS-PERSIST-011: Global navigation accessor feasibility
**Requirement text**: The exact accessor path for `ip_planet`, `in_orbit`, and related navigation globals shall be fixed before lifecycle/navigation coding depends on those globals.
