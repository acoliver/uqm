## Key Design Decision

The event pump goes INSIDE DoInput (not between activity dispatches) because:
1. DoInput is the universal per-frame function — every blocking loop calls it
2. TaskSwitch (the old yield-to-main-thread mechanism) has no other thread
   to yield to in single-threaded mode
3. TFB_FlushGraphics drains the DCQ every frame, preventing fill-up deadlock
