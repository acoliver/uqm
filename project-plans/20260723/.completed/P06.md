**Source**: `sc2/src/uqm/gameinp.c` lines 384-440.

### REQ-INJECT-002: Active-gate ordinal increment — PASS

**Source**: `rust/src/automation/input_ffi.rs` lines 64-98.

`rust_automation_service_do_input()`:
